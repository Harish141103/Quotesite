About BENJAMIN FRANKLIN

Benjamin Franklin was one of the Founding Fathers of the United States. He was an author, printer, politician, inventor, statesman, and diplomat. He played a key role in the American Revolution and helped draft the United States Constitution. Franklin was also a prominent scientist and inventor, known for his experiments with electricity.
