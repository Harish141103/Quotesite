About HENRY FORD

Henry Ford was an American industrialist, businessman, and founder of the Ford Motor Company, which revolutionized the automobile industry. He was born on July 30, 1863, in Greenfield Township, Michigan, and died on April 7, 1947, in Dearborn, Michigan.
