About HEDY LAMARR

Hedy Lamarr was an Austrian-born American actress, inventor, and scientist. She is best known for her film career in Hollywood during the 1930s and 1940s, but she also made important contributions to the field of wireless communication through her invention of a frequency hopping signal during World War.
