About JAMES WATT

James Watt was a Scottish inventor, mechanical engineer, and chemist who is best known for his work on improving the efficiency of the steam engine, a key technology of the Industrial Revolution. He is considered one of the most important figures of the Industrial Revolution and his work had a significant impact on the development of modern machinery and transportation.
