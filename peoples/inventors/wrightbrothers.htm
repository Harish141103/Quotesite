About THE WRIGHT BROTHER

The Wright brothers, Orville and Wilbur Wright, were two American aviation pioneers who are credited with inventing and building the world's first successful airplane. They achieved this feat on December 17, 1903, when they made four flights in a powered, heavier-than-air craft at Kitty Hawk, North Carolina.
