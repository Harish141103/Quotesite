About BILL BRADLEY

Bill Bradley is a former American professional basketball player, Rhodes Scholar, and politician. He played for the New York Knicks for 10 seasons, winning two NBA championships, and later served as a United States Senator from New Jersey from 1979 to 1997. He was also a candidate for the Democratic Party's nomination for President of the United States in 2000.
