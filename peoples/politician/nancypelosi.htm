About NANCY PELOSI

Nancy Pelosi is an American politician who has served as a member of the United States House of Representatives since 1987. She represents California's 12th congressional district and is a member of the Democratic Party. She has served as the Speaker of the House of Representatives from 2007 to 2011 and again from 2019 to present, making her the first woman in American history to hold that position.
