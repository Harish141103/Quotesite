About CHANAKYA

Chanakya, also known as Kautilya or Vishnugupta, was an ancient Indian philosopher, teacher, economist, and political advisor who lived in the 4th century BCE. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest minds in Indian history and is credited with writing the Arthashastra, a treatise on economics, politics, and military strategy.
