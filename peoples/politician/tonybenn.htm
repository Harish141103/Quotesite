About TONY BENN

Tony Benn (1925-2014) was a British politician who served as a Member of Parliament (MP) in the House of Commons for over 50 years. He was a member of the Labour Party and served in various high- profile positions, including as Secretary of State for Industry, Secretary of State for Energy, and President of the Labour Party.
