About INDIRA GANDHI

Indira Gandhi (1917-1984) was an Indian politician who served as the third Prime Minister of India from 1966 to 1977 and then again from 1980 until her assassination in 1984. She was the first and, so far, only female Prime Minister of India and played a crucial role in shaping India’s political and economic landscape.
