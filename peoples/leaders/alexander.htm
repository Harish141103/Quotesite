About ALEXANDER THE GREAT

Alexander the Great was a king of Macedon who lived in the 4" century BCE. He is considered one of the greatest military commanders in history, conquering much of the known world in his lifetime. Alexander was born in 356 BCE in Pella, the ancient capital of Macedon, and became king at the age of 20 after the assassination of his father.
