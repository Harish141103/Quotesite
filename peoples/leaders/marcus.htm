About MARCUS AURELLUS

Marcus Aurelius was a Roman Emperor who ruled from 161 to 180 AD. He is widely regarded as one of the most important Stoic philosophers and his book "Meditations" is considered a classic of ancient(Stoic literature.

Inspiring Quotes of
MARCUS AURELLUS

The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts.

Very little is needed to make a happy life, it is all within yourself, in your way of thinking.

The best revenge is to be unlike him who performed the injury.

Do not waste what remains of your life in pursuing your passions; do not be misted by the outward appearance of things.

The soul becomes dyed with the color of its thoughts:

The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way.

If it is not right, do not do it, if it is not true, do not say it.

The universe is change; our life is what our thoughts make it.

Accept the things to which fate binds you, and love the people with whom fate brings you together, but do so with all your heart.

Think of yourself as dead. You have lived your life. Now take what's left and live it properly.
