About JAWAHARLAL NEHRU

Jawaharlal Nehru was an Indian independence activist and the first Prime Minister of independent India. He played a key role in India's struggle for freedom from British rule and was a close associate of Mahatma Gandhi. Nehru was born on November 14,1889, in Allahabad, India, and served as Prime Minister of india from 1947 until his death in 1964.
