About DALAI LAMA

The Dalai Lama is the spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism and has been recognized as the leader of the Tibetan government in exile since 1959 The current Dalai Lama is Tenain Gyatso, who was born in 1935 and was recognized as the 14 Dalai Lama at the age of two.
