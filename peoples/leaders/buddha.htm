About BUDDHA

Buddha, also known as Siddhartha Gautama, was an ancient spiritual leader and founder of Buddhism. He was born in Nepal around the 5 century BCE and is believed to have attained enlightenment under a bodhi tree in India, which led him to become a teacher of the principles of Buddhism.
