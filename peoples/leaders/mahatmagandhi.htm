About MAHATMA GANDHI

Mahatma Gandhi was an Indian nationalist leader who is best known for his role in the Indian independence movement against British colonial rufe. He was borm on October 2, 1859, in Porbandar, a- coastal town in present-day Gujarat, India, Gandhi was a political and spiritual leader who believed in nonviolent civil disobedience and was the driving force behind India's independence from British rule. He was assassinated on January 30, 1948, in New Delhi.
