About MARTIN LUTHER KING IR

Martin Luther King Jr. was an American Baptist minister and civil rights activist who played a key role in the American civil rights movement from the mid-1950s until his assassination in 1968. He was born on. January 15, 1929, in Atlanta, Georgia, and became a prominent advocate for racial equality through nonviolent civil disobedience.
