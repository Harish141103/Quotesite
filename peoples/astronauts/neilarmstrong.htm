About NEIL ARMSTRONG

Neil Armstrong was an American astronaut and the first person to walk on the moon. On July 20, 1969, Armstrong became the first human to step onto the lunar surface as part of the Apollo 11 mission. His famous quote, “That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind,” has become an iconic moment in human history.
