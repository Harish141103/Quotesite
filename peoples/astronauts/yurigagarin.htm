About YURI GAGARIN

Yuri Gagarin was a Soviet Air Force pilot who became the first human to travel to space on April 12, 1961. His historic flight aboard the Vostok 1 spacecraft made him a national hero in the Soviet Union and a symbol of the possibilities of space exploration around the world. Gagarin continued to serve in the Soviet Air Force and was also active in promoting space exploration and international cooperation in the field.
