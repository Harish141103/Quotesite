About SCOTT KELLY

Scott Kelly is a retired American astronaut who spent a total of 520 days in space over four missions, including a year-long stay on the International Space Station (ISS) from 2015-2016. During his time in space, Kelly conducted a variety of experiments and research projects, including investigations into the effects of long-duration spaceflight on the human body. He is also a veteran of the U.S. Navy and a best- selling author.
