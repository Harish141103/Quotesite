About CHRIS HADFIELD

Chris Hadfield is a Canadian astronaut, engineer, and former Royal Canadian Air Force pilot who is best known for his time as the commander of the International Space Station in 2013. During his time in space, he gained a following for his social media presence, which included videos and photos showcasing life on the space station and the beauty of Earth from above. He has also authored several books about his experiences and works as a public speaker, advocating for science education and space exploration.
