About BUZZ ALDRIN

Buzz Aldrin is an American astronaut and engineer who was the Lunar Module Pilot on the Apollo 11 mission in 1969, during which he and Neil Armstrong became the first humans to walk on the moon. He has also been involved in space advocacy and worked to promote space exploration and colonization.
