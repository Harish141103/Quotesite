About ABBY WAMBACH

Abby Wambach is a retired American professional soccer player who is widely considered to be one of The greatest female soccer players of all time. She played for the US women's national soccer team for 14 years and helped the team win two Olympic gold medals and the FIFA Women's World Cup in 2015.
