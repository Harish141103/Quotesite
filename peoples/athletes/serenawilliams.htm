About SERENA WILLIAMS

Serena Williams is an American former professional tennis player who is widely regarded as one of the Greatest tennis players of all time. She has won numerous Grand Slam titles, including singles, doubles, And mixed doubles titles, and has been ranked world No. 1 in singles on multiple occasions.
