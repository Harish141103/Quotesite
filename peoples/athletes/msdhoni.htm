About MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a retired Indian cricketer who is widely Regarded as one of the greatest cricket captains in history. He played as a wicket-keeper and middle- Order batsman for the Indian national cricket team and led the team to numerous victories in major Tournaments, including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
