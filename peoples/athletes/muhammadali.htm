About MUHAMMAD ALI

Muhammad Ali, also known as "The Greatest", was an American professional boxer and cultural icon Who is widely regarded as one of the greatest athletes of all time. He won numerous championships and Awards throughout his career, and was also known for his activism and outspokenness on social and Political issues.
