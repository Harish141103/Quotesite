About VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national cricket team in all Formats. He is widely considered as one of the greatest batsmen in the world, and has numerous records To his name. Kohli has been the recipient of several awards, including the prestigious Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018.
