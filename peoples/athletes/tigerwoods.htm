About TIGER WOODS

Tiger Woods is an American professional golfer who is widely considered to be one of the greatest Golfers of all time. He has won numerous championships and awards throughout his career, including 15 Major championships, and has been ranked as the world's number one golfer for a record total of 683 Weeks.
