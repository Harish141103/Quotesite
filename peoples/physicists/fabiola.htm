About FABIOLA GIANOTTI

Fabiola Gianotti is an Italian physicist who is best known for her leadership role in the discovery of the Higgs boson particle, which was a major breakthrough in the field of particle physics. She is currently the Director-General of CERN, the European Organization for Nuclear Research..
