About ISAAC NEWTON

Isaac Newton was an English physicist, mathematician, and astronomer who is widely regarded as one of the most influential scientists in history. He is best known for his laws of motion and universal gravitation, which he developed during the late 17th century and laid the foundations for classical physics..
