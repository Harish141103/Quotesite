About ALBERT EINSTEIN

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who is widely considered as one of the most influential scientists of the 20th century. He is best known for his theory of relativity, mass-energy equivalence formula (E=mc²), and his groundbreaking contributions to the development of quantum mechanics...
