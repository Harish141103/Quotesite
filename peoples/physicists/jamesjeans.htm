About JAMES JEANS

James Jeans was a British physicist, astronomer, and mathematician who made significant contributions to the understanding of the universe and the physical laws that govern it. He wrote extensively on a wide range of scientific topics, and was also known for his ability to explain complex concepts in simple terms.
