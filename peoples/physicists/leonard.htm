About LEONARD MLODINOW

Leonard Mlodinow is an American physicist and author who has made significant contributions to the fields of theoretical physics and cosmology. He has written several popular science books, including “The Drunkard’s Walk: How Randomness Rules Our Lives” and “The Grand Design,” which he co-authored with Stephen Hawking..
