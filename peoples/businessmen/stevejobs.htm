About STEVE JOBS

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur and inventor, best known as the co-founder and CEO of Apple Inc. He was also instrumental in the development of personal computers, the iPod, iPhone, and iPad, and revolutionized several industries such as music, personal computing, and mobile communication.
