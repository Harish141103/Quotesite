About WALT DISNEY

Walt Disney was an American entrepreneur, animator, voice actor, and film producer. He co-founded The Walt Disney Company, which is one of the world's largest entertainment companies, and is best known for creating iconic characters like Mickey Mouse and for building the Disneyland and Walt Disney World theme parks.
