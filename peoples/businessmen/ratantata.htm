About RATAN TATA

Ratan Tata is an Indian businessman and philanthropist who served as the chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, from 1991 to 2012. He is widely recognized for his contributions to the growth and development of the Tata Group, as well as his humanitarian work.
