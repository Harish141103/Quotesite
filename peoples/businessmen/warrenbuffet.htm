About WARREN BUFFETT

Warren Buffett is an American business magnate, investor, and philanthropist. He is the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, a multinational conglomerate holding company. He is widely regarded as one of the most successful investors in history and is also known for his philanthropy, having pledged to give away the majority of his wealth to charity.
