About MARK ZUCKERBERG

Mark Zuckerberg is an American entrepreneur and co-founder of Facebook, one of the world's largest social networking platforms. Born on May 14, 1984, in New York, Zuckerberg attended Harvard University where he created the initial version of Facebook in 2004. Today, he is one of the richest people in the world and has become a prominent figure in the tech industry.
