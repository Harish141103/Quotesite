About A.P.J.ABDUL KALAM

APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11 President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and is widely known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contributions to the development of ballistic missile technology in India.
