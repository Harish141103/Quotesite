About MIKHAIL GORBACHEV

Mikhail Gorbachev is a Russian politician and statesman who served as the General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union from 1985 until 1991. He is credited with introducing significant reforms in the Soviet Union and helping to bring an end to the Cold War.
