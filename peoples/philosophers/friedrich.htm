About FRIEDRICH NIETZSCHE

Friedrich Nietzsche (1844-1900) was a German philosopher, poet, and cultural critic who is best known for his ideas on morality, religion, and the concept of the “superman.” He challenged traditional values and beliefs, encouraging individuals to create their own meaning in life.
