About LAO TZU

Lao Tzu (also spelled Laozi) is a legendary Chinese philosopher who is believed to have lived during the 6th century BCE. He is the author of the Tao Te Ching, one of the most important works of Chinese philosophy.

LAO TZU

“Nature does not hurry, yet everything is accomplished.”

“When I let go of what I am, I become what I might be.”

“A good traveler has no fixed plans and is not intent on arriving.”

“The wise man does not lay up his own treasures. The more he gives to others, the more he has for his own.”
