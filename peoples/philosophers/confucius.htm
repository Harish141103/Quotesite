About CONFUCIUS

Confucius (551 BC - 479 BC) was a Chinese philosopher and teacher whose teachings have had a significant impact on Chinese, Korean, and Japanese cultures. His philosophy emphasizes the importance of ethical behavior, moral values, and respect for authority.
