About BARACK OBAMA

Barack Obama is an American politician and attorney who served as the 44" President of the United States from 2009 to 2017. He was the first African American to hold the presidency, and during his tenure, he signed several landmark pieces of legislation, including the Affordable Care Act and the Dodd- Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act.
