About ABRAHAM LINCOLN

Abraham Lincoln was the 16th President of the United States, serving from 1861 until his assassination in 1865. He is widely regarded as one of America's greatest presidents, having led the country through its Civil War and helped to preserve the Union, abolish slavery, and modernize the economy.
