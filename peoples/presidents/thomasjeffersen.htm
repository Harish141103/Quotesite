About THOMAS JEFFERSON

Thomas Jefferson was one of the Founding Fathers of the United States, serving as the third President of the United States from 1801 to 1809. He was the principal author of the Declaration of Independence and was also known for his contributions to the fields of architecture, agriculture, and education.
